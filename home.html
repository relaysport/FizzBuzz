FIZZBUZZ

This challenge is based around the child's game, FizzBuzz, which is used to teach division. In this game, several players alternate counting up to 100 starting with one. For every multiple of three, the player says "Fizz" instead of the number; for every multiple of five, the player says "Buzz" instead of the number. When the number is a multiple of both three and five, the player says "FizzBuzz". My program uses HTML, JS, CSS, and Bootstrap to replicate FizzBuzz.
